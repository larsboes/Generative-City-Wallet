# Social Coordination: The Spark Wave

## Overview: Anonymous Momentum

Spark enables social coordination and crowd-building without requiring a persistent social graph or contact scraping. By aggregating anonymous co-movement signals, the system can provide honest social proof and collective incentives.

---

## 1. Anonymous Co-Movement Signals

The backend tracks anonymous active sessions that have accepted offers for the same merchant within a rolling time window (e.g., 30 minutes).

- **Momentum Framing:** If 3+ users are heading to a venue, future offers for that venue can include social proof (e.g., "3 Spark users are already on their way").
- **Privacy Boundary:** No individual identities or locations are revealed; only aggregate counts per merchant.

---

## 2. The Spark Wave Mechanic

The "Spark Wave" is a user-initiated social multiplier built on top of the Milestone Coupon system.

### How it works:
1. **Initiation:** A user accepts a milestone-type offer (e.g., "Discount fires at 50 guests") and generates a **Wave Link**.
2. **Sharing:** The user shares the link with friends (symmetric benefit).
3. **Catalyst Bonus:** If the friends join the wave and their redemptions contribute to hitting the merchant's milestone, the original sharer (the "Catalyst") receives an additional reward (e.g., double cashback).
4. **Verification:** Redemptions must be physical and verified by the merchant, preventing bot-based abuse.

---

## 3. Incentive Alignment

| Stakeholder | Benefit |
|---|---|
| **Merchant** | Receives a guaranteed "cluster" of arrivals to build atmosphere. |
| **Catalyst User** | Receives a bonus for coordinating their social group. |
| **Joining Friends** | Receive a high-relevance offer and social proof of the venue's upcoming state. |
